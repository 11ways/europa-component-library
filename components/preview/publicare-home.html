What's new 03 August 2017

Do you know about the EUR-Lex training courses?

For about 2 years the Publications Office has been offering a classroom training programme on EUR-Lex entitled ‘Make your search for EU law a success!’ for people working in EU institutions and agencies. As the programme has proved very successful, more courses are scheduled for this autumn. Take the opportunity to enrol now for one of the following courses.
