Atomic design patterns

The styleguide framework combines several commmon approaches of problem solving:

  • Atomic design patterns for breaking elements into atoms, molecules and organisms
  • BEM for naming elements in hierarchical way

For further details and examples, browse the corresponding sections in this styleguide or familiarize yourself with this blog post.
